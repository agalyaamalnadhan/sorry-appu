Oii Appu ❤️ Please read this slowly baby... Every word comes from my heart 🥺❤️

Namma first time pesina naal la irundhu, inniku varaikum nee en life-la vandhadhunaala than love na enna nu purinjuchu 🥺 Before you, life normal-ah than poitu irundhuchu… aana nee vandhadhuku apram every small thing kooda special-ah feel aagudhu. Morning text, late night calls, random fights, random laughs, silent moments… idhellam enakku maraka mudiyatha memories ❤️

Sometimes naan strong-ah iruken nu act pannuven… aana un kitta pesama iruka mudiyadhu. Un message vara late aana kooda, naan phone-ah thirumba thirumba paapen 😭 Nee “busy” nu sonnalum, en mind full-ah unna than miss pannitu irukum. Un voice ketta calm aaguren… un smile paatha stress marandhudum… un presence dhaan enakku peace ❤️

Oru sila neram, nee hurt aagiruka nu therinjium, naan ego la silent-ah irundhuruken. Adha nenacha ipo romba guilty-ah feel panren 🥺 Nee deserve panna reassurance, comfort, care, attention… adhula naan konjam late aayiten. Nee “naan avaluku important-ah?” nu feel panna start panni irrupa… and honestly, adhuku naan than reason. I’m really sorry baby ❤️

“Saptiya?” “Thoonguniya?” “Careful-ah iru…” indha simple words kooda nee en kitta expect pannirupa… Aana sila neram naan adha proper-ah kudukala. Nee chinna chinaa anbu-kaga engi irundha… aana naan full-ah purinjukaama poiten 🥺 Ippo adhellam yosicha, ennaku kastama irruku.

Nee kovama irundhalum, silent-ah irundhalum, distance create pannalum… en heart-ku nee than romba spl ❤️ Namma sanda potta days la kooda, night vandha odane “ipo nee enna panra?” nu than yosipen. Because nee en life oda oru important chapter ❤️

I know words alone everything fix panna mudiyadhu… Aana genuinely solren, naan improve aaganum nu aasai padren. Un feelings-ah innum better-ah purinjukanum… unakku comfort kudukanum… nee lonely-ah feel panna vidakudadhu. Nee deserve panra love-ah daily show panna try panren ❤️

Sometimes naan un kitta too attached-ah iruken… because unna miss paniduven nu yosichu bayama irukum 🥺 Un presence illa na everything empty-ah feel aagudhu. Songs kettalum needhan nyabagam vara… Entha places ponalum needhan nyabagam vara… Entha random things paatha kooda “un kuda irrutha memories” nu nenapu varudhu ❤️

Namma relationship perfect illa. Aana honestly, perfect-ah irukanum nu kooda enakku thevai illa… Because real-ah iruku ❤️ Real love-na sanda varum… misunderstanding varum… distance varum… tears varum… Aana ivlo nadandhalum vittu pogama irukuradhu than important 🥺

Thank you for staying with me even when I became difficult to love. Thank you for understanding me even when I couldn’t explain my feelings properly. Thank you for making me feel loved during my worst days ❤️

3 years mudinjudhu… Aana un mela iruka love korayala baby… daily konjam konjam adhigam than aagudhu 😭❤️ Innum neraya memories create pannanum… late night talks venum… random dates venum… small fights venum… apram adhuku apram hugs venum ❤️

Always you. Only you. Forever you. 💌


No matter how many fights come between us… No matter how hard days become… At the end of every single day, my heart still chooses only you ❤️
